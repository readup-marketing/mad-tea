A bright idea materialized in Alice's mind. "Is that why there are so many teacups and plates on this table?"

"Yes, that's it exactly," said the Hatter with a melancholic sigh. "It's always tea-time, and we never have time to wash the dishes between."

"So you keep moving around the table?" said Alice.

"Exactly," said the Hatter, "as we use up the clean ones."

But Alice was beginning to feel drowsy and bewildered from all this peculiar conversation. What would happen if she said something that upset them?
