"Yes, that's it," said the Hatter with a sad sigh. "It's always tea-time, and we never have time to wash the cups in between."

"So you keep moving around the table?" said Alice.

"Exactly," said the Hatter, "as we use up the clean ones."

But Alice was beginning to feel sleepy and muddled from all this odd talk. What would happen if she said something to upset them?
